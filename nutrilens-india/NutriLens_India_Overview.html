NutriLens India The Problem & Our Answer
Why We Built This

Indian food deserves Indian answers.

Every Indian eats idli, sambar, biryani, and dal every day — yet every calorie app in the market is built around pizza, burgers, and sushi. We fixed that.

The Problem

No Indian food data

Popular apps like MyFitnessPal have little to no accurate data for Indian dishes — especially regional ones like rasam, pongal, or tamarind rice.

Wrong serving sizes

Indian portions are unique — 2 idlis, a quarter plate of rice, a small katori of sambar. No app asks the right questions for the right food.

People give up tracking

When the app doesn't recognise "dosa" or "curd rice", users stop tracking altogether — leaving their health goals unmet.

Our Solution

1,014 verified Indian foods

Every entry comes from India's official INDB 2024.11 database — the National Institute of Nutrition's gold-standard data set.

Smart portion questions

After you pick a food, the app asks food-specific questions: "How many idlis?" or "Small, medium, or large biryani bowl?" — getting portion right.

Works for every meal

Breakfast idli, restaurant biryani, home-cooked dal, evening chai — NutriLens handles it all with precision and simplicity.

Why it matters

India has 77 million diabetics, 135 million obese adults, and a rising rate of lifestyle diseases — almost entirely linked to diet. Yet there is no accurate, accessible tool to help Indians understand what they eat daily. NutriLens India fills that gap.

NutriLens India Key Features
What It Does

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

NutriLens India was designed to be used at the dining table, not in a lab. Every feature serves one purpose: making Indian nutrition tracking effortless.

AI Photo Recognition

Powered by Google Gemini Vision. Just photograph your meal and the AI detects the dishes automatically — no manual tagging needed.

Largest Indian Food Database

1,014 items from INDB 2024.11 — the National Institute of Nutrition's official dataset. Covers all of India's regional cuisines.

Per-Food Portion Templates

Each food category has smart questions: "How many dosas?" or "Biryani bowl size?". Portions are calculated per-dish, not per-100g.

Live Nutrition Preview

As you select portions, the calorie and protein count updates in real-time — before you even confirm the food. See your meal's nutrition as you build it.

4 Health Goal Scores

Every meal is scored 0–100 for Weight Loss, Muscle Gain, Diabetic Friendly, and Heart Health — giving personalised, actionable feedback.

Meal History & Dashboard

Every meal is saved. Track your weekly calories, protein trends, top foods, and daily progress against your 2,000 kcal goal on a clean dashboard.

Also included

Quick Meal Templates Smart Food Suggestions Synonym Search (chai → tea) Micronutrients Detail Share Results Mobile-First Design 6 Micronutrients (Ca, Fe, Vit-C…) Meal Search & Filter

The App (Frontend)

A fast, mobile-first web application. Works on any phone browser — no app download required. Clean, intuitive design built for daily use at mealtimes.

The AI Brain

Google Gemini Vision identifies Indian dishes in photos with high accuracy. For manual entry, a smart search engine finds the right food from 1,014 options — even with spelling variants and local names.

The Nutrition Engine

When you answer the portion questions, the engine applies scientifically-calibrated multipliers to the INDB base values — giving you accurate nutrition for your exact serving, not just a generic average.
